Brain Tumor Segmentation

Using both public datasets and private datasets derived from collaborators and our own clinical trials, we use deep learning to perform automatic tumor segmentation in the case of glioblastoma and tumor metastases. We focus on turning our methods into open-source and publically-usable via Docker-enabled Python packages.

Project Links: DeepNeuro ● qtim_bmSegmenter ● Segmentation Wizard (3D Slicer) ● Latest Publication

Radiogenomics

We apply deep learning to MR imaging to non-invasively predict genomics in glioma, such as the presence of Isocitrate Dehydrogenase mutations. This non-invasive tool can serve as an alternative to invasive biopsy, guiding patient management at an earlier stage of disease and in follow-up.
